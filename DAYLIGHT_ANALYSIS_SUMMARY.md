## Comparison Summary

### The FKT Advantage
The 24/7 hiking strategy allows the FKT holder to:
- **Gain 9-10 hours per day** of additional hiking time
- **Hike during coolest hours** (night/early morning)
- **Avoid midday heat** in summer sections
- **Make up time** on easier sections by hiking longer

### The Daylight-Only Advantage
Most thru-hikers prefer daylight-only because:
- **Safety**: Better visibility for navigation and hazards
- **Social**: More interaction with other hikers
- **Recovery**: Proper 7-8 hours sleep for muscle recovery
- **Enjoyment**: See scenery, wildlife, views
- **Sustainability**: Lower injury risk, more sustainable pace

## Calculations Used

### Daylight Hours Formula
Based on solar declination and latitude:
```
daylight_hours = (2 √ó hour_angle) √∑ 15 + twilight
```

Where:
- Hour angle depends on latitude and day of year
- Twilight adds ~1 hour (civil twilight usable for hiking)

### Required Pace Calculation
```
required_pace = miles_per_day √∑ (daylight_hours √ó 0.75)
```

The 0.75 factor accounts for:
- Breaks (snacks, water, rest)
- Camp setup/takedown
- Navigation/route finding
- Non-movement time
